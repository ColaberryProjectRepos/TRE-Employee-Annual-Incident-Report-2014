This project about the transport and road accident occurred in Australia in 2014. The TMRE Employees Annual Incident dashboard provide the total accidents in each month, in quarters and breakdown the type of accidents.  And provide what kind of treatment has been given for each accident and also show the effect of the accident in different age group.
